• preety URL

    "!" tells the crawler that your site supports the AJAX crawling scheme:

    http://knapo.net/photos#!/photo/58-the-taste-of-malta

  • ugly URL - hash fragment becomes part of the query parameters:


    http://knapo.net/photos?_escaped_fragment_=/photo/58-the-taste-of-malta
  • facebook and google+ also support the AJAX crawling scheme for link sharing.
